Takeaways

  • Motivation:
    • Testing GR and its alternative on cosmological scales
    • Assess field-level information on gravity signatures
    • Prepare Bayesian forward models for Euclid exploitation
         Generalization to scale-dependent forward models
  • Results:
    • Beyond-GR reconstruction of the initial conditions of the Universe
    • Improved sensitivity to gravity model parameters
  • Method:
    • Fast and differentiable simulation code for GR,  f(R) and nDGP theories
    • Test study in a Bayesian hierarchical framework on BOSS-like data
